Filter reference

SampleDescription
field = value
field = "value"		 Objects where field = 'value'. Other operators supported are >, >=, <=, !=.
field like "foo.*" Case insensitive regex match. In this case look for documents where field starts with "foo". Note that the .* is needed.
field > 10 and field < 20 field is between 10 and 20. Combine basic expressions with and and or
(field > 10 and field < 20) or name = "bar" field is between 10 and 20 OR name is "bar". Parentheses provide priority as expected.
field in "foo,bar,baz" field is one of foo, bar, or baz. Also nin can be used for "not in".
Note the query parser attempts to convert values to numbers before sending to the backend.
To clear the query, push filter with an empty string.
